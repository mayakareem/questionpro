# Sample Queries for AI Research Guide

This document contains example questions you can ask the AI Research Guide to get methodology recommendations.

## Pricing Questions

- "What should we charge for our new premium tier?"
- "How do I determine the optimal price point for my SaaS product?"
- "What features justify a higher price in my product?"
- "How price sensitive are customers in the enterprise segment?"
- "Should we offer monthly or annual pricing, or both?"

## Feature Prioritization

- "Which features should we build next in our product roadmap?"
- "What product capabilities do customers value most?"
- "Should we add live chat or improve our help documentation?"
- "What's the priority order for these 10 feature requests?"
- "Which features drive conversion from free to paid?"

## Customer Satisfaction

- "How satisfied are our enterprise customers compared to SMB customers?"
- "What's driving customer dissatisfaction in our support experience?"
- "Are customers happy with our new onboarding process?"
- "How can we measure satisfaction across different touchpoints?"
- "What's causing low satisfaction scores in the mobile app?"

## Customer Experience

- "Why are customers churning from our service?"
- "Where are the biggest friction points in our customer journey?"
- "How can we reduce customer effort when they need support?"
- "Which touchpoints in our experience need the most improvement?"
- "What's causing drop-off during onboarding?"

## Brand & Market Research

- "How well-known is our brand compared to competitors?"
- "What do people think about our brand in the market?"
- "How should we position our product against competitors?"
- "What's the size of our addressable market for this new product?"
- "Who are our target customer segments?"

## Product Launch

- "Will this new product succeed in the market?"
- "What messaging resonates best with our target audience?"
- "Should we launch product A or product B first?"
- "What features are must-haves for a successful launch?"
- "How can we validate product-market fit before launch?"

## Loyalty & Retention

- "Will our customers recommend us to others?"
- "Who is at risk of churning and why?"
- "What drives customer loyalty in our industry?"
- "How can we increase customer retention?"
- "Why do customers choose us over competitors?"

## Competitive Analysis

- "How do we compare to our main competitors?"
- "What are our competitive advantages?"
- "Why do customers choose competitors instead of us?"
- "What gaps exist in the market that we can exploit?"
- "Where do we win and lose against competition?"

## A/B Testing & Optimization

- "Which homepage design will convert better?"
- "Should we use message A or message B in our campaign?"
- "What call-to-action performs best?"
- "Which email subject line gets more opens?"
- "What's the optimal flow for our checkout process?"

## Market Segmentation

- "Who are our ideal customer personas?"
- "What distinct customer segments exist in our market?"
- "How do customer needs differ across segments?"
- "Which segment should we target first?"
- "What characteristics define our best customers?"

## Mixed/Complex Questions

- "We're launching a new pricing tier with premium features—how do we validate the price, prioritize features, and identify the target segment?"
- "Our NPS scores are dropping—how do we figure out why customers are unhappy and what to fix first?"
- "We want to enter a new market—how do we understand customer needs, size the opportunity, and position against competitors?"
