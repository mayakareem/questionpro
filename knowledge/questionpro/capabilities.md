# QuestionPro Platform Capabilities

## Overview

QuestionPro is a comprehensive research platform that supports the full research lifecycle—from survey design to data collection to analysis and reporting. This document maps research methodologies to specific QuestionPro capabilities.

## Core Platform Features

### Survey Building & Distribution
- **Survey Builder**: Drag-and-drop interface with 30+ question types
- **Question Library**: Pre-built, certified questions for common research needs
- **Logic & Branching**: Skip logic, display logic, piping, and randomization
- **Multi-language Support**: Create surveys in multiple languages
- **Distribution Channels**: Email, web links, SMS, QR codes, embedded widgets, social media
- **Panel Access**: Reach target audiences through QuestionPro's panel network

### Advanced Research Modules

**Conjoint Analysis**
- Choice-based conjoint (CBC)
- Adaptive choice-based conjoint (ACBC)
- Market simulator for scenario testing
- Automatic calculation of part-worth utilities and feature importance

**MaxDiff Analysis**
- Best-worst scaling exercises
- Automatic balanced design generation
- Priority scoring and ranking
- Segment-level analysis

**Text Analytics**
- AI-powered sentiment analysis
- Theme extraction from open-ended responses
- Word clouds and visualizations
- Categorization and tagging

**CX Platform (CX Reputation)**
- Customer journey mapping
- Touchpoint tracking across the lifecycle
- Real-time feedback collection
- Closed-loop feedback workflows
- Alert routing for detractors

**NPS & Satisfaction Tracking**
- Pre-configured NPS surveys
- Automated scoring and dashboards
- CSAT and CES question templates
- Trend tracking over time

### Data Collection & Quality

**Sample Management**
- Access to global panel (millions of respondents)
- Custom audience targeting (demographics, firmographics, behaviors)
- Sample quality controls (attention checks, speeders, straightliners)
- Quota management

**Survey Experience**
- Mobile-optimized surveys
- Progress indicators
- Save-and-resume functionality
- Response validation

### Analysis & Reporting

**Real-Time Dashboards**
- Live response monitoring
- Pre-built visualizations (charts, graphs, tables)
- Filter and segment responses
- Export-ready reports

**Statistical Analysis**
- Cross-tabulation (crosstabs)
- Statistical significance testing
- Correlation and regression analysis
- Driver analysis (key drivers of satisfaction/NPS)

**Advanced Analytics**
- Trend analysis (wave-over-wave comparison)
- Segment comparison
- Conjoint market simulation
- Text analytics with AI

**Reporting & Sharing**
- Export to Excel, SPSS, CSV, PDF, PowerPoint
- Branded reports with custom styling
- Share dashboards with stakeholders
- API access for integration

## What QuestionPro Does Well

### Strong Capabilities
- **Survey distribution at scale**: Email, panel, multi-channel reach
- **Pre-built templates**: Faster setup for common research types (NPS, CSAT, pricing)
- **Real-time dashboards**: See results as they come in
- **Advanced methodologies**: Conjoint, MaxDiff, text analytics built-in
- **Data quality tools**: Attention checks, fraud detection, quality scores
- **Integration**: API access, CRM integrations, export to analysis tools

### Where You'll Need Workarounds
- **Qualitative depth**: For in-depth interviews, you'll conduct externally and use QuestionPro for recruitment and transcript analysis
- **Complex statistical modeling**: Advanced techniques may require exporting to SPSS or R
- **Video analysis**: Focus group videos analyzed outside QuestionPro
- **Custom dashboards**: Complex visualizations may need BI tools (Tableau, PowerBI)

## Common Workflows by Research Need

### "I need to understand customer needs"
→ Use: Survey Builder + Open-ended questions + Text Analytics
→ Output: Themes, sentiment scores, verbatim quotes

### "I need to prioritize features"
→ Use: MaxDiff Module
→ Output: Ranked feature list with importance scores

### "I need to optimize pricing"
→ Use: Van Westendorp PSM or Conjoint Analysis
→ Output: Optimal price point, acceptable price range, demand curve

### "I need to track brand health"
→ Use: Survey + Trend Reports + Competitive Benchmarking
→ Output: Awareness, consideration, preference trends over time

### "I need to improve customer experience"
→ Use: CX Reputation Platform + Driver Analysis
→ Output: Journey stage performance, key drivers of satisfaction, pain points

### "I need to test concepts"
→ Use: Survey Builder + Display Logic + Rating Scales
→ Output: Concept performance scores, purchase intent, qualitative feedback

### "I need to understand pricing + features together"
→ Use: Conjoint Analysis Module
→ Output: Feature importance, willingness to pay, market share simulation

## Platform Limitations & Considerations

**Sample Size Requirements**
- Most advanced methods (conjoint, MaxDiff) require 200-500+ responses
- Smaller sample sizes may need external recruitment beyond QuestionPro panel

**Technical Complexity**
- Conjoint and advanced analytics have a learning curve
- May need QuestionPro support or training for complex setups

**Qualitative Research**
- Platform is primarily quantitative
- Qualitative methods (interviews, focus groups) use QuestionPro for recruitment and text analysis, not moderation

**Custom Analysis**
- Complex statistical models may need export to specialized software
- Most common analyses are built-in

**Cost Considerations**
- Different features available at different pricing tiers
- Advanced modules (conjoint, CX, panels) typically require higher-tier plans

## Getting the Most from QuestionPro

### Best Practices
1. **Start with templates**: Use pre-built surveys when possible
2. **Pilot test**: Run with 20-30 responses before full launch
3. **Use quality controls**: Enable attention checks and fraud detection
4. **Leverage automation**: Set up recurring surveys for tracking studies
5. **Export strategically**: Download raw data for custom analysis when needed

### When to Use QuestionPro vs Other Tools
- **Use QuestionPro when**: You need to survey customers at scale, test pricing/features, track metrics over time
- **Supplement with other tools when**: You need deep qualitative insights (use Zoom + transcription), complex statistical modeling (export to SPSS/R), or specialized usability testing

## Summary

QuestionPro excels at **quantitative research at scale**—surveys, advanced methodologies like conjoint and MaxDiff, and ongoing tracking studies. It provides the infrastructure to recruit respondents, field surveys, analyze data, and share insights.

For **qualitative depth** (interviews, ethnography), **highly specialized analysis** (advanced modeling), or **real-time behavioral data** (product analytics), you'll use QuestionPro as part of a broader research toolkit.

The platform's strength is making sophisticated research methods accessible without requiring deep statistical expertise—most of the heavy lifting (design, analysis, visualization) is automated.
