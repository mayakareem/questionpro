# Example Research Questions by Industry

This document contains plain-English business questions that the AI Research Guide should be able to answer. Use these as test cases and examples for users.

---

## Banking & Financial Services

### Retail Banking
1. "Why are customers closing their checking accounts and switching to digital-only banks?"
2. "What features would make our mobile banking app more competitive with Chime and Current?"
3. "How do we reduce customer effort in the mortgage application process?"
4. "Should we charge for premium checking or keep it free to compete with online banks?"

### Commercial Banking
5. "Why are mid-market companies choosing fintech lenders over traditional banks?"
6. "What do CFOs value most when selecting a treasury management provider?"
7. "How satisfied are our business banking customers with our relationship managers?"

### Wealth Management
8. "What would make high-net-worth clients switch from their current wealth advisor to us?"
9. "How do clients perceive our brand compared to Fidelity and Schwab?"

## Using These Prompts

### For Testing
- Use these questions to validate that the AI returns appropriate research plans
- Check that recommendations match the methodology guides
- Verify QuestionPro workflows are correctly mapped

### For User Examples
- Show users the types of questions the tool can handle
- Demonstrate breadth across industries
- Illustrate different decision types (pricing, features, CX, competitive, etc.)

### For Training
- Use as few-shot examples in LLM prompts
- Include industry context to improve recommendations
- Show variety in question framing and specificity

## Question Patterns

### Pricing Questions
- "What should we charge..."
- "How much would customers pay..."
- "Should we price at X or Y..."

### Feature/Product Questions
- "Which features should we prioritize..."
- "What product should we launch..."
- "Why aren't people using X feature..."

### Customer Experience Questions
- "Why are customers churning..."
- "Where are customers struggling..."
- "How can we reduce effort/frustration..."

### Competitive Questions
- "Why do customers choose [competitor]..."
- "How do we compare to..."
- "What would make customers switch..."

### Market Understanding Questions
- "What do [target audience] value most..."
- "Is there demand for..."
- "How important is X versus Y..."

## Industry-Specific Considerations

### Banking
- High regulatory constraints
- Security and trust are critical
- Digital transformation vs traditional service

### Retail
- Omnichannel complexity
- Fast-moving consumer preferences
- Price sensitivity varies by segment

### Beauty
- Emotional purchase drivers
- Influencer and social media impact
- Clean/natural vs clinical/efficacy tension

### Telecom
- Commodity product with limited differentiation
- Network quality vs price trade-offs
- High switching costs but also high churn

### SaaS
- Freemium and tiered pricing complexity
- Product-led growth considerations
- Feature bloat vs simplicity

### Healthcare
- Patient experience vs clinical outcomes
- Insurance and cost transparency issues
- Trust and credibility paramount

Each industry has unique research considerations that should inform methodology recommendations.
